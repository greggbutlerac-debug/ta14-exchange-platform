# Claims Matrix

## Permitted
- “TA-14 identified route-level gaps under the stated specification.”
- “This is a self-declared AER for the stated route and version.”
- “The route received HOLD because the listed required proof was missing or stale.”
- “A Durable Route Record preserves official identity, timestamp, essential manifest, minimal history, and a stable lookup reference.”

## Conditional
- “Reviewable” only when formal examination inputs are complete.
- “Verified” only after an authorized independent evidence-based decision and while live in the registry.
- “Continuously verified” only when current evidence and controls maintain the basis under an active assurance scope.

## Prohibited
- “TA-14 certified/approved/endorsed this system” for an automated free workflow.
- Any claim that payment removed a finding or caused a favorable decision.
- Any claim that a TA14-RID proves all underlying assertions true.
- Any static badge that hides suspension, expiration, limitation, or revocation.

## Boundary language
Identifiers prove continuity of a bounded record. Truth remains dependent on evidence, methods, authority, and current status.
